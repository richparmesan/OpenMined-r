Aqueous Extraction : Panning

Panning Mining is a form of placer mining to extract gold/ore in a traditional way which utilizes a pan and water.

×

Explanation

Gold panning is a basic, hands-on method to extract denser gold from soil or gravel by washing it in a shallow pan filled with sand and gravel. The pan is shaken in water, causing the heavier gold to settle at the bottom. This technique is commonly used to recover gold from stream beds, especially in the bends of streams and on the bedrock shelf, known as placer deposits.
