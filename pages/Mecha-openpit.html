Mechanical Extraction

Open Pit

-Process of extraction of near surface deposit by means of a surface pit. It is used when ore deposit is in shallow depth and little overburden needs to be removed. Both the overburden (if present) and the ore are typically removed in benches that vary from 30 ft (9 m) to 100 ft (30 m) in height.

- Open-pit mines are often divided into benches, with each bench representing a level where mining operations take place. Benches are progressively dug deeper into the earth to extract ore.
